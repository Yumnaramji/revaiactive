Our Story

Built for the way you move.

We believe great activewear should be high quality, affordable, designed to fit all bodies, and versatile enough to meet every individual's definition of movement. That's exactly what we set out to build.

Founded 2026

"Kit for that moment. And every session after it."

Based between the UK and Kenya. Built for everyone.

REVAÍ started from a straightforward desire — to find activewear that was genuinely high quality, honestly priced, and built to move with you no matter what movement looks like in your life. Not just for one sport, one body type, or one level of fitness. For all of it.

Movement means something different to everyone. For some it's five sessions a week. For others it's a Sunday walk, a yoga class, or a round of golf. REVAÍ is designed around that reality — kit that performs wherever you take it, fits across a full size range, and doesn't ask you to spend a fortune to get quality that lasts.

We're based between the UK and Kenya, we launched in 2026, and we're building something we're genuinely proud of. Every piece is designed to perform, fit honestly, and last — whatever your version of movement is.

What we stand for

01

Built on Innovation

Every REVAÍ piece is engineered with purpose. From compression-mapped construction to squat-proof fabrics and moisture-wicking technology — we bring technical performance to every garment. Each design goes through real-life athlete testing and field research before it reaches you, so what you're wearing has already been proven in motion.

02

Built for Everyone

S through XXL on every style. Whether you're doing your first 5K or your fifth marathon, whether you train at 5am or 9pm — this kit is for you, not a curated ideal of who an athlete should be. We actively work with professional athletes, personal trainers, gym regulars, and everyday active people of all shapes and sizes to make sure REVAÍ works for everyone.

03

Uncompromising Quality

We refuse to cut corners. Every seam, every stitch, every fabric choice is made to last. Our garments go through rigorous testing standards globally — meeting and exceeding international performance benchmarks — to deliver top-of-the-range quality that performs the first time you wear it and the hundredth time.
