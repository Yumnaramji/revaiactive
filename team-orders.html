Team Order Benefits

Volume Discount

15% for 10–24 pieces. 20% for 25–49 pieces. 25% for 50+ pieces. Discount applies across all tiers and mixed orders.

Custom Heat Transfer Printing

Add your club logo, team name, or number to any piece. Heat transfer printing sits flat against the fabric, stretches with the garment, and won't affect moisture-wicking performance. Setup fee applies once per logo.

Dedicated Account Manager

A named contact at REVAÍ handles your order from quote to delivery. Same-day responses during business hours. No holding queues.

Flexible Payment

Pay by card, bank transfer, or invoice (net-30 for qualifying organisations). 50% deposit on orders over 50 pieces, balance on dispatch.

Team Order FAQs

The minimum for team order pricing and benefits is 10 pieces. These can be any combination of products, sizes, and colours across any tier. A mixed order of 6 running tights and 4 training tops qualifies, for example.

Standard orders (no custom printing) typically dispatch within 3–5 business days and arrive within 2–3 days domestically. Orders with custom heat transfer printing require an additional 3–5 business days for production. Rush orders can sometimes be accommodated — discuss your timeline with your account manager.

Yes — mixed orders are the norm. You can specify different sizes for different team members, and mix product types (e.g., some members receiving tights and others receiving shorts). Your account manager will help you put together a size sheet template to collect requirements from your team.

We accept vector files (SVG, AI, EPS, PDF) for best results. High-resolution PNG or JPEG (300dpi+) also work for most logos. If your file format isn't listed, send it anyway and our design team will assess. A one-time setup fee applies per logo, then your design is stored for all future re-orders.

Yes. Every item in a team order is covered by our standard 60-Day Performance Guarantee. Claims for team orders can be submitted in bulk by the account holder — contact your account manager or email info@revaiactive.com with the relevant details and photos.
