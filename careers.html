How we work

Three values.
One way of working.

We keep things flat, honest, and focused. There's no politics and no posturing. Just people who care about making something genuinely good.

Move Together

We work as a team, not in silos. Decisions are made in the open and everyone's perspective counts, regardless of seniority. If you have an opinion, say it. If you disagree, say that too.

Make It Better

Good enough is never the goal. We constantly question whether what we've made can be improved — in fabric, in process, in the way we talk to customers. If you see something that could be better, fix it or flag it.

Own Your Work

We give people real responsibility from day one. There are no approval chains for everything and no one looking over your shoulder. You're trusted to do excellent work and to ask for help when you need it.

What we offer

Perks worth
mentioning.

No ping-pong tables. No free kombucha. Just things that actually matter to people building careers.

Full kit allowance

€500 annual allowance to spend on REVAÍ gear, plus a 40% staff discount on all purchases. Because we make it, you should wear it.

Remote-first options

Many roles are fully remote. Copenhagen-based roles offer 2 remote days per week as standard. We care about output, not office hours.

Health & wellbeing

Comprehensive health insurance for all full-time staff, plus a monthly wellbeing budget of €80 to spend on gym, physio, mental health apps, or anything that keeps you well.

Generous leave

30 days holiday plus public holidays. 4 months fully paid parental leave for all parents. No approval required for up to 5 days sick leave per year.

Growth budget

€1,000 per year for courses, conferences, books, or any learning that makes you better at your work. No approval process — just let your manager know what you're doing.

Flat structure

No unnecessary management layers. Everyone has direct access to the founders. Decisions are made quickly and with the people most affected by them in the room.
