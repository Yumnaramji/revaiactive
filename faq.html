Sizing & Fit

It depends on what you're training for. For running or HIIT, go smaller for better compression. For strength training, go larger for freedom of movement. For yoga and pilates, go true to size. Check our full Fit Guide for detailed training-type recommendations.

Sizing & Fit

Our Core line runs true to size. The Performance and Pro lines use a more athletic cut — slightly slimmer through the shoulders and thighs. If you're new to REVAÍ and ordering Performance or Pro, we recommend measuring yourself and comparing to the size chart on our Fit Guide page.

Sizing & Fit

All REVAÍ garments are available from XS to XXL. Our XXL is a genuine XXL — we don't vanity size. We're actively working on extending our range to 3XL and 4XL and expect to launch extended sizing in Q3 2026. Join our mailing list to be notified when it's available.

Care

Fabric softener works by coating textile fibres with a lubricating layer. In performance activewear, this coating blocks the moisture-wicking channels built into the fabric, reducing its ability to move sweat away from your body. Even one or two washes with fabric softener can cause noticeable degradation in moisture management. Use a sports-specific detergent instead.

Care

After every training session. Leaving sweat in activewear fabric — even in antimicrobial pieces — causes odour compounds to bond with the fibres over time. The more promptly you wash your kit, the longer it will smell fresh and perform well. At minimum, air your kit out immediately after use if you can't wash it straight away.

Sustainability

Sustainability is something we take seriously and are actively working towards. Our current fabrics are not made from recycled materials, but sourcing responsibly is a core part of our roadmap. Full details are on our Sustainability page.

Sustainability

Our outer shipping boxes are 100% recycled cardboard and fully recyclable. The inner garment bag is made from 100% recycled paper. We eliminated single-use poly bags from our packaging in 2024. The only exception is the resealable bag used in some Pro-tier items for transit protection — we're actively working on a paper alternative.

Sustainability

We offset 100% of our direct shipping emissions through verified carbon removal projects. We're working towards full Scope 3 emissions transparency and published our first supply chain emissions report in 2025. Our target is net-zero across all operations by 2030. We believe in transparency over greenwashing — if you want the details, our full sustainability report is available on request.

Sustainability

We run a take-back programme for worn REVAÍ garments. Send any REVAÍ piece back to us (postage paid label available on request) and we'll ensure it's either recycled through our textile recycling partner or repurposed. Items in wearable condition are donated to community sports programmes. We're building a closed-loop system — your old kit helps make new ones.
