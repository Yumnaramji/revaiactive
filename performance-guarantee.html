Performance Guarantee

30-Day Performance Guarantee

If it doesn't perform, we make it right. Every REVAÍ piece is backed by our performance guarantee for 30 days from the date of purchase — because we're confident in what we make.

What's Covered

Any failure that affects the performance of your garment within 30 days of purchase is covered — no receipt needed, no lengthy claims process. The item must not have been washed to be eligible.

Seam Failure

Any seam that separates, frays, or fails during normal training within 30 days of purchase. This includes flatlock seams, bonded seams, and stress seams at the crotch and underarm.

Compression Degradation

If your compression tights, shorts, or base layers lose noticeable elasticity and no longer provide the intended muscle support — even after correct washing and drying.

Fabric Breakdown

Pilling, thinning, or structural breakdown of the main fabric body that cannot be attributed to incorrect care or physical abrasion damage.

Zipper Failure

Any zipper that splits, jams, or becomes non-functional under normal use within 30 days of purchase. Covers all zipped pockets, half-zips, and full-zip jackets.

What's Not Covered

We're fair about this. The guarantee covers manufacturing and material failures — not general wear, misuse, or the natural ageing of any garment.

—

Accidental damage

Cuts, tears, burns, or abrasion damage caused by contact with sharp surfaces, rough terrain, or equipment.

—

Wrong size selection

Seam stress caused by wearing a size too small for your body is not covered. Please use our fit guide before ordering.

—

Normal colour fade

Gradual lightening of colours over many washes is a normal property of all dyed textiles and is not covered. Significant, rapid fading within 30 days of purchase is covered.

—

Washed items

Items that have been washed are not eligible for the performance guarantee. To make a claim, the item must be unwashed. If you have already washed the item, it falls outside the guarantee terms.

—

Care label non-compliance

Damage resulting from tumble drying, hot washing, bleaching, or other care that contravenes our guidelines is not covered.

—

Claims after 30 days

The guarantee period is 30 days from date of purchase. Claims submitted after 30 days will be assessed case by case at our discretion.

How to Claim

Three steps. Most claims are resolved within 48 hours.

1

Photograph the issue

Take clear photos of the defect from two angles — one showing the whole garment and one close-up of the problem area. Good photos speed up the review significantly.

2

Contact our team

Email support@revaiactive.com with your order number, photos, and a brief description of when the issue occurred. You can also submit via the contact form on this site.

3

Receive your replacement

We'll review your claim and dispatch a replacement within 48 hours of approval — no need to return the faulty item in most cases. If a refund is preferred, we'll process that instead.
