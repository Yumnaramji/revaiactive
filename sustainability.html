Sustainability

Built to last. Shipped with care.

We're committed to making decisions that are good for people and the planet. Here's what that looks like at REVAÍ right now.

What we do now

Packaging — fully plastic-free.

Every REVAÍ order ships in 100% plastic-free, recyclable packaging — because how we send things matters as much as what we send.

No Plastic Mailers

We use paper-based mailers only. No poly bags, no bubble wrap.

Fully Recyclable

All packaging — boxes, mailers, and inserts — goes in standard recycling.

No Unnecessary Inserts

No filler tissue, no excess branded collateral, no single-use plastic stickers.

Minimal Materials

We use only what's needed. Every packaging decision is made with waste reduction in mind.

Every order, packed with purpose.

From the moment you order to the moment it arrives — we've thought about the impact. Plastic-free packaging is just the beginning of how we think about what we put out into the world.
