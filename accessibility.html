Accessibility

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment

REVAÍ is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone — regardless of ability, device, or assistive technology. We aim to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

What We've Done

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy throughout all pages
  • Meaningful alt text on all product and content images
  • Keyboard-navigable menus, modals, and interactive components
  • Focus indicators visible on all interactive elements (:focus-visible styling)
  • ARIA labels on icon-only buttons (cart, search, menu close)
  • Sufficient colour contrast ratios across text and backgrounds
  • Reduced-motion media query respected for users with vestibular sensitivities
  • Responsive design that adapts from mobile to desktop without horizontal scrolling

Ongoing Work

Accessibility is never truly "done." We are continually reviewing and improving our site, including:

  • Regular automated audits using Lighthouse and axe DevTools
  • Manual testing with screen readers (NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Keyboard-only navigation testing across all flows

Known Limitations

Some embedded third-party content (such as video embeds) may not fully meet our accessibility standards. We are working with our vendors to improve this over time.

Feedback & Contact

If you experience any barriers while using our site, or have suggestions for improvement, please get in touch:

  • Email: info@revaiactive.com
  • We aim to respond to accessibility-related enquiries within 3 business days.
