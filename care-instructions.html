Care Instructions

Keep your kit performing.

REVAÍ fabrics are engineered to last — but they need the right care to stay that way. Follow these guidelines and your kit will perform wash after wash.

Quick Reference

At a glance care guidance for all REVAÍ garments. Full details for each category are below.

Care Step Symbol Instruction Temperature / Setting
Washing 🫧 Machine wash — gentle / delicate cycle Cold — max 30°C / 86°F
Drying 💨 Hang dry or lay flat to dry Do not tumble dry
Ironing 🚫 Do not iron Heat damages stretch fibres

Detailed Care Guide

🫧

Washing

✓

Wash cold at 30°C / 86°F

Hot water breaks down elastane fibres and reduces compression performance over time.

✓

Turn garments inside out

Protects the outer surface from pilling and preserves colour vibrancy.

✓

Use a gentle / delicate cycle

Reduces mechanical stress on seams and the fabric structure.

✗

No fabric softener

Fabric softener coats moisture-wicking fibres and eliminates their sweat-management properties within a few washes.

✗

No bleach or optical brighteners

These degrade both the colour and the structural integrity of technical fibres.

💨

Drying

✓

Hang dry where possible

Hang on a rail or over a drying rack. Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods, which can fade colours.

✓

Lay flat for compression pieces

Hanging tights or compression shorts by the waistband while wet can cause the waistband to stretch unevenly.

✗

Do not tumble dry

Heat from tumble drying causes elastane fibres to lose their elasticity permanently. Even a low-heat setting will shorten the garment's lifespan significantly.

✗

Do not wring or twist

Gently squeeze excess water instead. Wringing distorts the fabric structure.

📦

Storage

✓

Fold compression pieces — don't hang them

Hanging compression tights and shorts on hangers over long periods causes the waistband to lose tension. Fold and store flat in a drawer instead.

✓

Store in a cool, dry place

Avoid damp gym bags for extended periods. Moisture trapped in fabric can encourage odour-causing bacteria even in odour-resistant fabrics.

✓

Tops and jackets can be hung

Tops, jackets, and non-compression pieces can be stored on hangers without issue.

✗

Don't leave in a gym bag for days

Even antimicrobial fabrics benefit from being aired out after use.

⚗️

Special Treatments

ℹ

Silver-ion antimicrobial fabrics

Present in our Performance and Pro lines. Avoid washing with chlorine bleach, which deactivates the silver-ion treatment. Use a sports-specific detergent for best results.

ℹ

Thermal regulation fabrics (Pro line)

The phase-change material in Pro thermal pieces is embedded in the fibre — not a coating — so it won't wash out. However, washing above 40°C can reduce effectiveness over time.

ℹ

DWR water-repellent finish

Our running jackets use a DWR (Durable Water Repellent) coating. This can be refreshed after washing by briefly running a warm iron (not directly on the fabric — use a pressing cloth) or tumbling on a no-heat setting for 10 minutes once every 10–15 washes.

ℹ

Mesh and perforated panels

Use a mesh laundry bag when washing garments with mesh panels to prevent snagging against zips or velcro on other items.

Understanding Care Labels

Every REVAÍ garment has a care label. Here's what the standard symbols mean.

🫧

Tub with water

Machine washable. Number inside = max temperature in °C.

💧

Dripping square

Do not tumble dry. Hang or lay flat to dry.

🔲

Square with circle

Tumble dry. An X through it means do not tumble dry.

⚠️

Triangle

Bleaching guidance. Crossed triangle = no bleach of any kind.

All REVAÍ care labels also include written instructions in English. If a label has worn or faded, refer to this page or contact us at support@revaiactive.com.
