Fit Guide

Find Your Fit

Finding the right fit shouldn't be complicated. Use this guide to get the most out of every piece — whether you're racing, lifting, or recovering.

Measurement Guide

Measure over your base layer or skin for the most accurate result. Use a soft tape measure and keep it parallel to the floor.

How to measure

📐

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level across your shoulder blades.

📏

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest point of your torso, usually just above the navel.

🔵

Hips

Stand with feet together. Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat, roughly 20cm below your waist.

📊

Inseam

Measure from the top of your inner thigh to the bottom of your ankle bone while standing straight.
